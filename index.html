Atish Kumar

PhD Candidate in Economics, IIIT Delhi

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at IIIT Delhi and a Visiting Scholar at the Safexpress Centre for Data Learning and Decision Sciences (SCDLDS), working under the supervision of Prof. Sandeep Juneja.

My research focuses on computational economics and finance. I am simultaneously working on empirical finance, macroeconomic modelling, and climate modelling for policy evaluation.

Prior to my PhD, I worked as a Senior Risk Analyst at the Hong Kong and Shanghai Banking Corporation (HSBC).
