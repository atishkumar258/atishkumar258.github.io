Computational economics and finance, with applications to macro and climate.

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at IIIT Delhi and a Visiting Scholar at the Safexpress Centre for Data Learning and Decision Sciences (SCDLDS), working under the supervision of Prof. Sandeep Juneja. My research focuses on computational economics and finance, with an emphasis on developing and applying machine learning techniques to economic data. I am currently working on empirical finance, macroeconomic modelling, and climate modelling for policy evaluation.

Research interests

  • Empirical macroeconomics and finance with a focus on asset pricing, macro–finance linkages, and business cycle dynamics.
  • Machine learning for economic data, including nonparametric and model-based approaches.
  • AI in climate modelling for evaluating climate risks and policy interventions.

Background

Prior to my PhD, I worked as a Senior Risk Analyst at the Hong Kong and Shanghai Banking Corporation (HSBC) in India. This experience informs my current work on financial risk, macroeconomic uncertainty, and climate-related stress testing.
