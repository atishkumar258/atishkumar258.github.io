About

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at IIIT Delhi and a Visiting Scholar at the Safexpress Centre for Data Learning and Decision Sciences (SCDLDS), working under the supervision of Prof. Sandeep Juneja.

My research focuses on computational economics and finance, with an emphasis on developing and applying machine learning techniques to economic data. I am currently working on empirical finance, macroeconomic modelling, and climate modelling for policy evaluation.

Prior to my PhD, I worked as a Senior Risk Analyst at the Hong Kong and Shanghai Banking Corporation (HSBC) in India.

Research interests

  • Empirical macroeconomics and finance
  • Machine learning for economic data
  • AI in climate modelling and policy

Current work

I am developing methods and applications at the intersection of macroeconomics, financial risk, and climate policy. Ongoing projects include empirical analysis of asset markets, macroeconomic models with rich data, and the use of machine learning techniques in climate impact assessment.
