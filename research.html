Job Market Paper

Quantifying Economic Damages from Climate Change Using Conditional Diffusion-Based Projections

with Sandeep Juneja, Manmeet Singh, Dhruman Gupta

This paper develops a conditional diffusion modeling framework to produce probabilistic projections of climate variables that are consistent with both historical observations and future scenarios from empirically validated climate models for India. Using these projections, we estimate the economic damages from climate change for India under various emission scenarios.

Market Power and Core Inflation: Evidence from India

with Kiriti Kanjilal

Firm pricing behavior can have major macroeconomic implications, including high inflation. We leverage firm-level balance sheets and granular core inflation data to study the relationship between profitability metrics such as markup and market power of firms and core inflation in India. We observe a negative relationship between markups and core inflation, which echoes the theoretical relationship in the New Keynesian Literature.

Anomaly Boosting: A Deep Learning Approach

with Sandeep Juneja, Nagpurnanand Prabhala, Aman Foujdar, Sidharth Wagle

We construct factors related to Indian equity markets leveraging annual firm balance-sheet and daily stock level data. In addition, we propose a novel approach to portfolio optimization by training a deep learning model using Fama-French factors. Our trading strategy generates an economically significant alpha and is easily implementable in practice.
